Questions?

I already have a website with online ordering.

Great — we integrate with your existing site or replace the ordering layer entirely. You keep your brand, your domain, and your customer data. No starting from scratch.

How is this different from UberEats or DoorDash?

They take 15–30% commission and own your customer relationship. OrderPortal is your own branded ordering channel — zero commission, full data ownership, and direct contact with every customer.

I'm a small restaurant. Is this worth it?

Absolutely. Even a restaurant with 100 regular customers can dramatically grow revenue just by reaching those people directly: a push notification on a slow night, a birthday reward, a new menu item announcement. We built this for independent operators first.

What does it cost?

A flat monthly subscription with no commission, no hidden fees, no contracts. Most restaurants save more in commission in their first month than the entire year of OrderPortal costs.

How long does it take to build?

Every restaurant is different, but we move fast. We handle everything: menu, branding, payment setup, and launch. You'll be surprised how quickly it comes together.

What if my customers don't download the app?

There's nothing to download. Your ordering page works in any browser. Customers tap a link and order — no app store required.
